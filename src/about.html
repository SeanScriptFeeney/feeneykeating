About Feeney Keating LTD

Feeney keating was founded in 1992 by Colm Feeney and Jarlath Keating. From it's inception, the company specialised in precision engineering, custom built fabrication, special purpose machinery, tool making, prototyping and product building equipment. Feeney Keating Ltd's is a Galway based Irish company with over 25 years in the business. They work with clients from all sizes not only in the Irish market, but also in International markets such as Europe and America.
The intention and policy of Feeney Keating Ltd is to produce work and service to a high quality standard and use quality measurement and production equipment to guarantee accuracy and precision to produce a quality product. Feeney Keating Ltd has implemented a quality system and achieved an ISO 9001 – 2008 Standard.
