Drawings & CAD

Our pre-production team work with both supplied hard copy drawings and CAD files in many formats and we can offer advice on conceptual design if or when required. At the initial stages of a project, our experienced engineers are often able to advise on ways to improve the manufacturing process and, in some cases, even reduce production costs of a machined item or the components that it forms part of. After production, our inspection team use the supplied drawings or CAD output as part of our quality control procedures.
